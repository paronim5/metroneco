Author: Dmitry Glukhovsky

Short Description

It's post-apocalypse Moscow. After the nuclear holocaust, a new fear is born, underground The year is 2033, the world has been reduced to rubble, and humanity is nearly extinct, half-destroyed cities having become uninhabitable through radiation. Beyond their boundaries, they say, lie endless burned-out deserts and the remains of splintered forests. Man has handed over stewardship of the earth to new life-formsmutated by radiation, they are better adapted to the new world. A few score thousand human survivors live on in the Moscow Metrothe biggest air-raid shelter ever built. Stations have become mini-statelets, their people uniting around ideas, religions, water-filtersor the simple need to repulse an enemy incursion. It is a world without a tomorrow, and feelings have given way to instinctthe most important of which is survival, at any price. VDNKh, the northernmost inhabited station on its line, remains securebut now a new and terrible threat has appeared. Artyom, a young man living in VDNKh, is given the task of penetrating to the heart of the Metro, to the legendary Polis, to alert everyone to the awful danger and to get help. In his hands he holds the future of his native station, the Metro, and perhaps the whole of humanity.

Characters

Character Artyom Metro 2033 © source

Artyom

He is a young man born shortly before the nuclear war that ravaged the world, and he has spent his life in the Moscow Metro. In the first game, Metro 2033, Artyom embarks on a perilous journey to warn the Metro's inhabitants about a looming threat from creatures known as the Dark Ones

Character Hunter Metro 2033 © source

Hunter
Character Description

n Metro 2033, Hunter is a high-ranking Ranger of the Order, known for his expertise and willingness to confront threats to the Metro system. He plays a crucial role as the catalyst for the game's main quest, tasking Artyom with a dangerous mission to save the entire Metro. Hunter's character is complex, driven by a self-interested desire to protect the Metro, even if it means resorting to violence.

Character Alex Metro 2033© source

Alex

is Artyom's adoptive father and mentor and a high ranking official in the VDNKh metro station

Character Bourbon Metro 2033© source

Bourbon

Bourbon is a key character and Artyom's first companion, a smuggler encountered early in the game/novel. Bourbon offers to guide Artyom to Polis in exchange for Artyom's help. However, their journey is cut short when Bourbon is killed by a mysterious, deafening noise emanating from the metro's pipes, which Artyom survives. This event leads to Artyom meeting Khan, who becomes his next guide.

Character Khan Metro 2033© source

The Mystic (Khan)

Khan in Metro 2033 is a mysterious and enigmatic character, a "wanderer" and mystic who plays a significant role in the story. He is known for his knowledge of the Metro's paranormal phenomena and his strong, charismatic personality. He acts as a guide and mentor to the protagonist, Artyom, particularly in navigating the mystical and dangerous aspects of the Metro
